## Headline

## Short Description

ColonyZero is a 2D idle incremental game where you tap a barren planet into a thriving colony. Mine minerals, construct buildings, grow your population, and when you've built everything there is to build — prestige into New Colony+ and start over with a permanent edge.

---

## Full Description

You land on an empty rock with nothing but a planet to click and a dream.

**Click the planet. Earn minerals. Build something.**

Every tap extracts minerals from the surface — your first foothold in a place that was dead five seconds ago. It's not much, but it's yours.

Spend those minerals on your first building. Then your second. Each structure you place starts working for you automatically, generating Minerals, Energy, and Oxygen around the clock. Step away, come back, watch the numbers climb. That's the loop — and it's more satisfying than it has any right to be.

**Six structures. Three resources. One goal.**

To survive on this rock you'll need to build out your entire colony — all six structures, each one unlocking a new layer of production:

- **Mining Rig** — raw mineral extraction, the backbone of everything
- **Solar Panel** — keeps the lights on and your energy reserves full
- **Biodome** — produces the oxygen your growing colony needs to breathe
- **Habitat Module** — housing for colonists, the living heartbeat of your base
- **Research Lab** — pushes your science forward and boosts colony efficiency
- **Orbital Launcher** — the crown jewel, because every great colony needs a way off the planet

As you build, colonists arrive. More colonists mean bigger planet clicks, faster growth, and a colony that starts to feel genuinely alive.

**The win condition is just the beginning.**

Once you've got all six buildings up and five colonists on the ground, your colony is declared complete. But instead of rolling credits, ColonyZero asks you a better question:

*What if you started over — but kept the momentum?*

**New Colony+** is the prestige system that rewards you for resetting. Wipe your buildings and minerals, walk away with a permanent +10% production multiplier, and build everything again — but faster. Stack prestige after prestige and watch each new run accelerate beyond what felt possible in the last one. The colony that took you an hour on the first run? You'll rebuild it in minutes.

**Your colony never stops working.**

Close the game, go touch grass, come back hours later — ColonyZero tracks up to 8 hours of offline earnings and drops a summary in your lap the moment you return. Every building you owned kept producing. Your colony didn't wait around.

## Key Features

- **Idle incremental gameplay** — active clicking when you want it, passive income when you don't
- **6 unique buildings** — each with distinct resource production and escalating costs
- **Three-resource economy** — balance Minerals, Energy, and Oxygen as your colony grows
- **New Colony+ prestige system** — reset for a permanent stacking +10% production multiplier
- **Offline earnings** — up to 8 hours of passive income calculated on every launch
- **Procedural audio** — all sound effects are synthesised at runtime, no audio files required
- **Persistent save system** — your progress is saved automatically every 30 seconds
- **Single-scene architecture** — fast load, zero fuss
